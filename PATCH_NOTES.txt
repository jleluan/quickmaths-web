QuickmathsWebDepo source-only patch

This archive intentionally DOES NOT include:
- package.json
- package-lock.json
- vite.config.ts
- tsconfig.json
- node_modules
- dist

Copy this archive over the root of your existing QuickmathsWebDepo working tree.

Controls:
- Left click: select/add block value
- Middle click: choose target/equal block
- Right click: unused for gameplay

Important fixes:
- Falling blocks can be selected and targeted, matching the original C++ game.
- Middle click replaces original right-click gameplay action.
- Scoreboard added: time, score, +pending score, high score, multiplier.
- High score persists via localStorage.
- Blocks can fall again when support below them is removed.
- This score tallies into the main score after the original-style delay.
